## 🔧 **Comprehensive Adjustment Test**

Try this command to test multiple properties at once:

```
"Design a competition rocket: 7cm diameter, 80cm long carbon fiber body, ogive nose cone, 4 swept fins with 5 degree cant angle, I-class motor, smooth finish, NASA white and blue colors"
```

This will adjust:
- ✅ Body diameter (7cm)
- ✅ Body length (80cm) 
- ✅ Material (carbon fiber)
- ✅ Nose shape (ogive)
- ✅ Fin count (4)
- ✅ Fin geometry (swept)
- ✅ Fin cant angle (5°)
- ✅ Motor type (I-class)
- ✅ Surface finish (smooth)
- ✅ Colors (white/blue)

## 🎨 **Visual Verification**

After each command, you should see:
- 🔄 **Immediate visual updates** in the 3D model
- 📊 **Property changes** reflected in component data
- 🎯 **Color changes** applied to materials
- 📐 **Dimension changes** in geometry
